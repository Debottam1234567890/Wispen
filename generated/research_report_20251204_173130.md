Key events and processes during this period include:

*   **Consolidation of British Power:** The British Raj implemented centralized administration, legal systems, and infrastructure projects (like railways and irrigation) primarily designed to serve British interests. This period also saw the systematic exploitation of India's resources, deindustrialization, and the suppression of indigenous industries.
*   **Rise of Indian Nationalism:**  British rule, despite its exploitative nature, also inadvertently sowed the seeds of Indian nationalism.
